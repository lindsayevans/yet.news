lolwut?

Allows you to create a basic single-page website that answers any question (as long as it ends in 'yet?') with a simple Yes/No/Maybe

Why?

I went through a period of manually creating a bunch of sites that did just that, but it seems like too much work to register the domain, create the page, setup hosting etc.

What's this 'Chaos mode' business?

When switched to 'On', anybody can change the answer to your question at any time

What about the password?

If you want to change your answer: make a note of the auto-generated password, or change to something more memorable

What are the rules?

I reserve the right to delete anything at any time for whatever reason
